A note on our outcomes data

On the road to success

A career extends well beyond that first big job offer.

Our part-time Data Analytics, Data Science, and Software Engineering students add an average $17k(1) to their salary after graduating. And they continue to earn raises and promotions more than a year after that.

Download our latest outcomes report to see the average student's job growth after graduation and beyond. Our research is ongoing: if you don't see recent results for your program, check back for our next update.

1. Results reflect a Thinkful online survey conducted among Thinkful graduates who reported an in-field job between September 2018 and February 2020. Respondent base (n=232) among 336 graduate invites. [Sample size represents this population of customers within a margin of error of 6.4% at 95% confidence.] Survey responses are not a guarantee of any particular results as individual experiences may vary. Survey fielded between February 16th and February 28th, 2021. Graduates invited to the survey were offered a $25 gift card.
